01 - Regional Network

How people, goods, and investment move through the region

The regional system includes interstate corridors, border ports, rail, airport facilities, transit routes, local streets, and bike and pedestrian infrastructure.

Corridors

Highway Network

I-10 I-19 I-8
  • I-10 connects Tucson to Phoenix, Los Angeles, El Paso, and Houston.
  • I-19 links Tucson to Nogales and the US-Mexico trade corridor.
  • Freight movement depends on truck access, interchange capacity, inspection capacity, and pavement condition.
Trade

Ports of Entry & Rail

Mariposa Port Port of Tucson Union Pacific
  • Mariposa Port of Entry is a major US-Mexico commercial gateway.
  • Nogales is a major rail gateway by loaded container volume.
  • Port of Tucson supports inland intermodal and Foreign Trade Zone activity.
Aviation

Airport Network

TUS Ryan Airfield Marana Regional
  • Tucson International Airport anchors commercial and cargo aviation.
  • Ryan Airfield and Marana Regional support general aviation demand.
  • Airport assets support passenger service, cargo, maintenance, defense activity, and general aviation.
Mobility

Public Transit System

Sun Tran Sun Link Sun Van
  • Sun Tran provides fixed-route bus service across metro Tucson.
  • Sun Link connects the University of Arizona, Downtown, and 4th Avenue.
  • Paratransit and shuttle services help fill regional mobility gaps.

04 - Key Agencies

Who plans, funds, and builds the regional network

Planning, funding, project delivery, and operations.

PAG

Pima Association of Governments

Federally designated MPO for greater Tucson. Prepares long-range transportation plans, secures federal and state dollars, and manages regional planning strategies.

RTA

Regional Transportation Authority

Independent state taxing district within Pima County. Collects and administers the half-cent sales tax through a board of regional officials.

ADOT

Arizona Department of Transportation

State agency overseeing I-10, I-19, and all state routes, while coordinating with PAG on prioritization and project planning.

Sun Tran

City of Tucson Transit

Primary mass transit operator for metro Tucson, including fixed-route bus, Sun Link streetcar, and Sun Van paratransit services.

TAA

Tucson Airport Authority

Operates Tucson International Airport and Ryan Airfield, supporting aviation, cargo, customs processing, and regional connectivity.
