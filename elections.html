03 - Ballot Outline

What is voted on

Primary

  • Party nominees for federal, state, legislative, county, and some local offices.
  • Precinct committeemen and party-specific contests where applicable.
  • Independent voters generally request a party ballot for partisan primaries.

General

  • Governor/lieutenant governor, statewide offices, U.S. House, Legislature, county offices, and judicial retention.
  • Citizen initiatives, legislative referrals, local propositions, bonds, overrides, and special-district funding.
  • Municipal contests depend on each city or town's election cycle.
